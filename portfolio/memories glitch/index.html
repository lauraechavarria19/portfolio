memories glitch

Have you ever thought about how important memories are?

Haven't you? Well, I'll explain: they reflect our past, but over time, the reflections can fall into oblivion. This glitch is an exploration of those moments that remain, but have changed shape: images full of noise, with flashes of clarity in the midst of chaos. It represents the fragility of memory and the strength of emotions that survive. Here you will find memories disarmed, distorted, but deeply human.
