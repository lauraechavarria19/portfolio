music is happiness

In the concerts she attended, Laura learned something very valuable: music and emotions are not fleeting. Even if the sounds fade away at the end of each show, the emotions they awaken in people remain engraved in their being. The feeling of belonging to something great, of feeling your heart beat to the rhythm of a song that completely envelops you, stayed with her long after the event was over. Somehow, those songs were like echoes of other fragmented memories, but at the same time they were new memories, like pieces of a half-finished puzzle.
